## Common Issues & Fixes

### Camera not working?
- Check browser permissions
- Use Chrome/Edge
- Ensure HTTPS (automatic on Render)

### Low scores?
- Improve lighting
- Stand further back
- Show full body in frame
- Wear fitted clothing

### Model loading slow?
- First load takes 2-3 seconds (normal)
- Subsequent loads are instant (cached)
